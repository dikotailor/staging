Tailoring Classes

Pricing & Timing

  • $100 / day (half the price of other tailoring clases)
  • 9am - 2pm (with a break in the middle)

What to Expect

In 6 months you will go from being a beginner to being ready to serve your own clients, through one on one guidance from a master tailor.

You’ll learn everything from the basics, like cutting pants, to more advanced techniques such as adding pockets and even learning how to make a custom suit. Get hands on experience from a master tailor.

What You Will Learn

Jackets

  • Shortening Sleeves
  • Adding/Removing Shoulder Pads
  • Shortening Collar
  • Waist Tightening & Loosening

Pants

  • Shortening
  • Waist Tightening & Loosening
  • Thigh Tightening & Loosening
  • Changing Zipper

Button Down Shirts

  • Shorten Sleeves
  • Adding/Removing Shoulder Pads
  • Shortening Collar
  • Waist tightening & Loosening

Specialties

  • Tightening, Loosening & Shortening Wedding Dresses
  • Tightening, Loosening & Shortening Seasonal Dresses

The Fundamentals

Each class we teach will require you to learn the fundamental tailoring tools, like the sewing machine and the finger thimble, as well as fundamental techniques such as professional cutting and sewing.

Every class is hands on.

If there’s any other technique you’re curious to learn, feel free to ask - Diko is willing to share any of his skills to any of his students.
