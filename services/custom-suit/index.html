Our Process

All work is done locally. Unlike many other tailors (who outsource their work to factories in other countries) our master tailor does everything in his shop.

Not only does this mean your suits are handled by an expert but it also means you have the power to provide your input or feedback at any point in the process.

Here’s what to expect after requesting a custom suit:

1. Measurements & Fabric Selection

Upon walking in:

  • You pick fabric from our sample
  • You pick the style of your costume (slim, regular, fit to body, etc.)
  • Our tailors take measurements

2. Hand & Machine Sewing

  • Our tailors hand cut the foyard made of the fabric you chose
  • Our tailors sew the suit together through precise stitching

3. Fitting

  • You come to try the suit, and our tailors will make note of adjustments to make sure the suit fits perfectly around your body.
  • As the last step, our tailors press the suit to remove wrinkles
  • You walk out with a handmade, truly custom, high quality suit

If you have questions about the process, please email us or call us and we will gladly clarify.
